About HUMSS Strand

The Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) track is designed for students who are interested in pursuing careers in social sciences, communication, education, or human resource management. This strand focuses on subjects that develop critical thinking, communication, and analytical skills.

Core Subjects

  • Introduction to Philosophy of the Human Person
  • Trends, Networks, and Critical Thinking in the 21st Century
  • Understanding Culture, Society, and Politics
  • Creative Writing / Oral Communication

Opportunities After Senior High

Graduates of the HUMSS strand can pursue courses in psychology, education, political science, communication, social work, and other related fields.
