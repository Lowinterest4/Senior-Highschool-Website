About STEM Strand

The Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) track is designed for students who are interested in careers related to science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. It emphasizes analytical, technical, and problem-solving skills.

Core Subjects

  • General Mathematics / Pre-Calculus
  • Basic Calculus / Statistics
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology
  • Research in Daily Life / Applied Science

Opportunities After Senior High

Graduates of the STEM strand can pursue courses in engineering, medicine, computer science, information technology, architecture, and other related fields.
