Admission Process

We welcome new students to our Senior High School. Follow the steps below to complete your admission.

Step 1: Requirements

  • Copy of Grade 10 Report Card
  • Birth Certificate
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character

Step 2: Application Form

Fill out our online or printed application form. Make sure all information is complete and accurate.

Step 3: Interview / Assessment

Attend an interview or assessment with our admission officers (online or on-site).

Step 4: Enrollment

Once accepted, submit the required documents and pay the enrollment fees to secure your slot.

*For further inquiries, please contact our admissions office via email or phone.
